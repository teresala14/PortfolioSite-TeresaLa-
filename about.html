About

My Story

My name is Teresa La and I am a UX/UI designer based in the NYC area. I graduated from Rutgers University with a degree in marketing because I found that the psychology of the mind of the consumer fascinating. I leveraged that knowledge in the restaurant business as a manager while utilizing social media and marketing tactics to increase turnover during Covid-19 when small businesses were struggling to adapt. At the same time I freelanced as a marketing analyst, identifying possible business opportunities.

During that time, I discovered UX design and wanted to pursue the field and began my training with Career Foundry. I learned techniques for design, research, information architecture, and how to create overall intuitive experiences. In my free time, I developed a love for cooking after a long history in the food industry and I’m constantly trying out new recipes. When I’m not working you can find me with a new book, in the kitchen, or outside walking my German Shepherd.
